About Me

I am a recent engineering graduate from Ryerson University. I studied mechanical engineering, specializing in mechatronics. My professional interests lie in embedded systems, automation, robotics, and mechanical design. I have experience with project coordination, 3D modeling, programming, control systems and more.

Projects

Haptic Device

Capstone project involving the design and development of a haptic device that can interact with a virtual environment. SolidWorks was used to design the physical device and some parts were 3D printed. Motors and encoders were connected to a Quanser Q8 DAQ which in turn was connected to QUARC real-time software. The program is built on top of SimuLink, it controls the hardware and allows for the creation of a virtual environment.

Linear Follower

A rack and pinion based unit that tracks a object along a straight line. The follower block stays at a constant 10cm from the leading block controlled by the user, with a accuracy of +/- 1mm. The project consisted of a motor and a ultrasonic distance sensor that had to be calibrated, with a National Instruments DAQ connected to LabView.

3-wheeled Mobile Robot

A robot car programmed in Python that uses kinematics for control, involving path planning, trajectory planning, and obstacle avoidance. MATLAB was also used to analyze and visualize data from the encoders of the wheels.
